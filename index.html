Itinerary Plan for 10 Days in the United Kingdom

If you're looking to spend a few days in the United Kingdom, keep reading! We'll spend a few days in Scotland, Wales, and England (most of the time will be spent in a few cities throughout England) :D

I took a flight to London Heathrow that landed early in the morning (around 7:30) so I opted to complete the bulk of heavy travelling in the first half of my trip. That being said, after I landed, I took a 4 hour train to Edingburgh, Scotland and worked my way from the northern parts of the UK back to London, England.
